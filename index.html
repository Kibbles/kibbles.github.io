March 21st 2020 - 10:00PM EST

Governor Whitmer issued an Executive Order to close a range of nonessential businesses. At this time, please do not leave your home if you do not absolutely need to, and take increased precautions around elderly, sick or immunocompromised individuals. Whenever possible, use Physical / Social Distancing when interacting in public spaces.
