Unofficial Monroe, Michigan COVID-19 Resources

Last updated March 21st, 2020

This page is an independent endeavour to collect news and support resources for citizens suffering economic or health-related harm due to the ongoing COVID-19 epidemic.

This page is not affiliated with the City of Monroe, Michigan.

If you would like to add resources or file a correction, please contact me via email.

March 21st 2020 - 10:00PM EST

Governor Whitmer issued an Executive Order to close a range of nonessential businesses. At this time, please do not leave your home if you do not absolutely need to, and take increased precautions around elderly, sick or immunocompromised individuals. Whenever possible, use Physical Distancing when interacting with larger public gatherings.
